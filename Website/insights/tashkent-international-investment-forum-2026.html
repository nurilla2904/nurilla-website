5th Tashkent International Investment Forum — 16–19 June 2026

2026-06-16

Uzbekistan’s flagship investment event returns for its fifth edition, bringing together heads of state, ministers, multilateral institutions and international investors across four days of plenary sessions, sector panels, pitch sessions, bilateral meetings and document signings.

What is TIIF?

The Tashkent International Investment Forum is Uzbekistan's highest-profile annual investment event, convened under the auspices of the President. Now in its fifth year, the forum is the primary platform for presenting Uzbekistan's investment pipeline to the international business community.

Programme overview

  • Day 0, 16 June (Pre-Opening). Exhibition opens. Panels on global investment trends, green transition, renewable energy finance, public-private partnerships and digital transformation.
  • Day 1, 17 June (Grand Opening). Presidential speech and high-level panel. Sessions on trade facilitation, tourism, SME financing, smart cities. Document signing ceremony.
  • Day 2, 18 June. Sessions on IPO strategies, private equity, SOE reform, mining, fintech, textiles and the chemical industry.
  • Day 3, 19 June (Closing Day). Sessions on transport and logistics, creative economy, legal frameworks, ESG, AI, healthcare, SEZs, automotive and smart construction. Closing ceremony.

Participation fees

  • USD 300 for payments received on or before 20 April 2026
  • USD 500 for payments received from 21 April to 5 June 2026

Making the most of TIIF

I support international companies at every stage: stakeholder mapping, bilateral meeting scheduling, on-the-ground logistics, interpretation and post-forum follow-up with government counterparts and potential partners.
